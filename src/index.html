When you click "Start Recording", this window will minimize, the whole screen will be recorded, and it will include the audio coming from both the computer and from the environment (e.g. your voice). The video will appear below when you click "End Recording". Then, when you click the "Download Video" button below, you will be shown a "Save As" prompt to download your recorded video. If you un-minimize this window while the video is recording, then the recording will pause, and this window will minimize again when you start recording again.
